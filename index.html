How it works

A simple workflow for transforming raw meeting conversations into clear decisions and practical action.

Capture

Upload audio, paste a transcript, or connect a meeting workflow after participant consent.

Understand

AI identifies topics, decisions, concerns, assumptions, action items, and open questions.

Evaluate

The system audits rationale, missing evidence, risks, contradictions, and agenda drift.

Improve

Receive better alternatives, next steps, follow-up questions, and a decision-quality score.
