Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

DecideWise AI is an early-stage product focused on privacy-first meeting intelligence. This website collects only the information that a visitor voluntarily shares through email or an optional lead form integration.

Information we may collect

When you contact us, we may receive your name, email address, organization, use case, and message. If you participate in a product pilot, meeting data will be handled only under a separate consent and pilot agreement.

How we use information

We use contact information to respond to demo requests, early-access requests, collaboration inquiries, and pilot discussions.

Meeting data

Meeting recordings, transcripts, or confidential documents should not be shared without participant consent. DecideWise AI is designed around consent, limited processing, and secure handling of meeting information.

Data sharing

We do not sell visitor contact information. If a third-party form provider is configured, submitted information may be processed by that provider according to its own privacy terms.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact founder@decidewiseai.me.
